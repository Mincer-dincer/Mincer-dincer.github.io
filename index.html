Essay Part 1

As I walk outside my house for my regular stroll, the sun blankets the horizon with a mellow glow. The traffic is down to a minimum with people strolling through the streets as I head to Ocean parkway. I bask in the warm sun as I walk down ocean parkway recollecting both the present and the past as I am submerged in my own thoughts. The warm hug this street provides me anchors myself to the ground. Be it the white noise of the cars driving by, the distant chirping of birds in the trees, an elderly couple in the comfort of each other, the group of bikers that go down their bike lanes, the occasional group playing chess. Street by street as I observe the world around me I get a glimpse of others' lives. It's comforting, serving as an anchor in my life in this everchanging city. It feels comforting in a way through thick and thin this place has stuck with me and has stuck with a lot of people. Be it the gathering of reunions and hangouts. Everywhere you look there is a story to be told. Every bench has a meaning to someone out there, be it where they used to hang out with friends or how they continue to sit and observe the cars passing by, it helps me calm down walking down recollecting my thoughts. As I have grown this street has helped me grow, comforting me as I walked back home, watching over me as I hung out with friends new and old, comforted me in times of regret and sorrow, and helped me move on.

Essay Part 2

As I walk down these streets I see before me familiar faces as they look back at me. I see the tree I crashed my bike into when I was 7(ouch), The street where I met one of my best friends to this day, The bench I used to play chess with a few elderly people. Even now I see stories being made memories being made, be it the first time they rode a bike, the first time they met a friend, confessing to a long time crush, Its as if ocean parkway serves as a time capsule trapping these memories in a state of stagnant state for me to look back to. Yet as I reminisce to these memories people are making new ones for themselves in front of me everywhere I look I see people bonding together experiencing stuff that will be forever engraved in their memories. Each one of these people have their own stories, memories , conflicts and accomplishments and now are intertwined with this very place. Not only has this street comforted me it has comforted many serving as a place of solitude and comfort in the goliath of a city New York is. Every piece of every story you see engraved in the places down these streets could resonate with so many.
